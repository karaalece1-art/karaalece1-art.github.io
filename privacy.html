Nova Mergeverse respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Nova Mergeverse does not collect personal data such as name, email, or contact details.

  • Device type and operating system
  • Gameplay interactions and usage data

This information is used solely to improve the app experience.

Advertising

Nova Mergeverse uses third-party advertising services, including Google AdMob, to display ads.

AdMob may collect certain data such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • Interaction with advertisements

For more information, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Nova Mergeverse offers optional in-app purchases such as coins and boosters. All purchases are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. We do not store payment information.

Children’s Privacy

Nova Mergeverse does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
